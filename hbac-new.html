What we do

Our service is designed to help the owners and curators of Scheduled Ancient Monuments (SAM), Listed buildings, Registered Parks and Gardens and non-designated heritage assets to understand the origin, historic development and significance of the sites and structures under their care.

Archaeology

HB Archaeology & Conservation Ltd provide a range of archaeological investigations including watching briefs, evaluations and excavations directed by Diccon Hart.

Interpretive Survey

Pioneered by David and Barbara Martin, this form of record is thorough and well illustrated and is designed to demonstrate the origin and evolution of a building or group of buildings.

Heritage impact assessments

A Heritage Impact Assessment is required for any application that directly affects a heritage asset or its setting

Heritage statements

The Heritage Statement is a dominant feature of the planning process. To prepare such a statement, the site is visited and an illustrated report prepared.
